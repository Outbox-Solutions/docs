## Overview

The **Opportunities** tab is where you **log and manage opportunities** in one place. Organize them into **pipelines**, move contacts through **pipeline stages**, and keep sales motion visible alongside your AI agents.

Use it for anything you would track as a deal or next step: booked demos, quotes sent, contracts out, and closed won or lost.

## Pipelines and stages

- **Pipelines** – Separate funnels for different products, teams, or campaigns (for example “Inbound” vs “Outbound”).
- **Stages** – Steps within a pipeline (for example *Qualified* → *Proposal* → *Won*). Move opportunities forward as conversations progress.

<Tip>
  Align stage names with how your team already talks about deals so reporting and handoffs stay obvious.
</Tip>

## Tools: Create & Update Opportunity

Add these from the **Tool Library** on voice or chat agents so the AI can **create** a new opportunity or **update** an existing one (stage, value, notes, etc., depending on configuration).

See the [Tool Library](/tool-library) for full details and the [Voice Agents](/voice-agents) / [Chat Agents](/chat-agents) guides for attaching tools.

---

## Workflow actions: Create & Update Opportunity

In [Workflows](/workflows), use **Create Opportunity** and **Update Opportunity** to drive pipeline updates from automation—for example after a high-score call, a Meta form submit, or an inbound webhook.
