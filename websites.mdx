## Overview

The **Websites** tab is where you **create AI-generated websites** for **landing pages**, campaigns, and lightweight marketing sites—without building from scratch in a separate builder.

Describe what you need, refine the result, and ship pages that match your offers and brand use cases.

---

## AI-generated pages

Outbox AI helps you produce page structure and copy so you can move from idea to a published page quickly. Use generated sites for:

- **Lead capture** – Dedicated URLs for ads, SMS, or voice follow-up
- **Campaigns** – One-off promos, events, or seasonal offers
- **Simple marketing** – Explainers, FAQs, or “book a call” landers

<Tip>
  Iterate in the Websites editor: tighten headlines, swap sections, and adjust CTAs until the page matches your campaign.
</Tip>

---

## Custom domains

Publish sites to **custom domains** so links look like your brand (or your client’s) instead of a generic host.

Configure domain settings in the Websites flow as prompted by the product—DNS verification, SSL, and routing steps are handled so visitors reach your live site securely.

<Note>
  Exact domain setup steps depend on your DNS provider; follow the in-app checklist when connecting a domain.
</Note>
