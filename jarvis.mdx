## Overview

J.A.R.V.I.S is your all-in-one AI operations hub inside Outbox AI. It helps you create and edit agents, build and update workflows, and pull campaign stats for calls and chats from one central place.

## Typical J.A.R.V.I.S Workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define Campaign Goal">
    Start with the objective (book appointments, re-engage no-answer leads, qualify inbound traffic, etc.).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate or Update Assets">
    Create or edit the required agents and workflows from one hub so everything stays aligned.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Campaign">
    Activate campaign automations and start processing contacts across voice and chat channels.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Performance">
    Use J.A.R.V.I.S stats views to monitor outcomes and make iterative improvements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Keep Agent and Workflow Changes Together" icon="link">
    When you update campaign messaging, also review related workflow conditions and follow-up actions to avoid mismatches.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Iterate in Small Batches" icon="flask">
    Roll out changes to a subset of traffic first, validate performance, then expand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Use Stats to Drive Prompt Updates" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Review call and chat outcomes regularly and feed that data back into prompts, tools, and branching logic.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
